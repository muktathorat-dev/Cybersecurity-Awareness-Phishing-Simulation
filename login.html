💡 Key Defensive Indicators:
1. Verify the Domain URL: Always inspect the browser's address bar. Official services do not host authentication pages on local hosts or unverified third-party environments.
2. Analyze Urgency: Phishing vectors often rely on artificial urgency (e.g., immediate password expiration) to bypass logical validation.
3. Report Suspicious Activity: Utilize internal security reporting mechanisms when an external email requests immediate credential entry.
